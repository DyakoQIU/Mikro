Our journey with Mikro

At Mikro, our mission is driven by a vision to revolutionize the everyday lives of the citizens of the Kurdistan region and Sulaymaniyah city. We recognized the many untapped opportunities present in these areas and the need for pioneers in technology to address them. With a commitment to impactful innovation, we set out to tackle these challenges head-on.

  • Our journey began with a profound understanding of Kurdistan and Sulaymaniyah's unique needs, leading us to pledge the creation of user-friendly solutions.
  • Understanding the region's aspirations, we vowed to deliver world-class technology tailored to enhance daily life experiences in Kurdistan and Sulaymaniyah.
  • Driven by the desire to empower the people, our journey commenced with a commitment to develop minimalist yet impactful products catering to Kurdistan and Sulaymaniyah's demands.

At Mikro, we take a holistic approach to designing technology. We strive to create user-centric solutions that simplify daily tasks and enhance the quality of life for everyone in the region. From improving connectivity and access to essential services to advancing digital transformation in business and personal use, we are dedicated to making a difference."
